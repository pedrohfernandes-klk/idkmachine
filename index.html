> IDK MACHINE · IDEA DIAGNOSTIC LOG > STATUS: operational, usefully uncertain > IDEA SEEDS: 666 IDK MACHINE is a one-button engine for beginning. It completes WHAT IF YOU... with a strange, usable prompt: a scene, rule, object, ritual, warning label, social mechanism, or small impossible assignment. It is not here to finish the work for you. It is here to make the next move easier, stranger, and harder to ignore. Each card is concrete enough to grip and open enough to bend. Use it as a title, first line, character problem, essay hook, game mechanic, image brief, worldbuilding rule, or wrong door worth following. Press the button when the page stalls, when a project feels over-explained, or when the obvious route has become too obedient. Copy what sparks. Ignore what does not.

> IDK MACHINE v2.0 · IDEA DIAGNOSTIC LOG > STATUS: operational, usefully uncertain > IDEA SEEDS: --- IDK MACHINE is a one-button ideation terminal for blank pages, stalled projects, strange scenes, essays, games, worlds, titles, characters, and any thought that needs a first unnatural spark. It does not deliver finished answers. It produces beginnings: sharp premises, visual constraints, odd conceptual engines, and half-open doors. Each card is designed to be specific enough to start movement and open enough to be stolen, bent, rewritten, or ignored. Press WHAT IF YOU... when the mind goes blank. The machine completes the question; you decide whether the result is a scene, a title, a character, a rule, a world, a metaphor, or a wrong turn worth following. > CERTAINTY ....... deliberately off > POSSIBILITY ..... high, unstable, renewable > METHOD .......... question first, explanation later > SIDE EFFECTS .... unusual premises, better questions, sudden movement > BEST USED FOR ... drafts, sketches, titles, scenes, worlds, essays, games Copying a card keeps the full question intact, because the idea begins with WHAT IF YOU... Nothing leaves this machine. Your idea deck is stored locally in your own browser and forgotten the instant you clear it. No account, no server, no audience but you. © HRF 2026.
